BATTLESHIP

Rules

  1. Select number of ships for each player.
  2. Each player places ships on their board. Press "R" to rotate.
  3. Players take turns choosing a cell to fire. Press "SPACE" to pause the turn.
  4. A hit damages a ship; a miss does nothing.
  5. A ship is sunk when all its cells are hit.
  6. The first player to sink all enemy ships wins.
